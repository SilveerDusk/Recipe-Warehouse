Nana's Special Cheesecake

Lastly, my favorite dessert! I recently just learned how to make this one. It is my Nana's Special Cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 12 Onces Cream Chesse
  • 2 Tbs. Rich Milk
  • 1 Tsp. Lemon Juice
  • Half of a Lemon Rind Grated
  • 2 Eggs, Beaten
  • Dash of Salt
  • Dash of Nutmeg

Chrust

  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups Crushed Graham Crackers or Vanilla Waffers
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 1 Cup Sour Cream

Add Filling Ingredients

Add Crust Ingredients

Preperation

  1. Let cream cheese soften at room temperature
  2. Once cream cheese is softened, beat with the milk and add the next 5 ingredients, 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp. vanilla; blend
  3. Mix crumbs and butte, and press in 9 inch pipepan. Put cheese mixture in unbaked crust and make in moderate oven at 375 degs for 20 minutes
  4. Let cool 1/2 hour
  5. Make topping of sour scream, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  6. Speard topping over cool cake and return to very hot overn at 450 degs for 5 minutes
  7. Note: I usually use prepared graham cracker crusts and grate a little bit more lemon rine than needed
