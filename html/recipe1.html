Santa's Workshop Pancakes

The Santa's Workshop Pancakes are a family breakfast classic. If you're feeling fancy you can even use molds!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 3 Tbs. Sugar
  • 2 Tsp. Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp. Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp. Salt
  • 2.5 Cups Buttermilk
  • 4 Tbs. Unsalted Butter Melted
  • 1/2 Tsp. Vanilla Extract
  • Nonstick Spray
  • Syrup for Serving

Add Ingredients

Preperation

  1. Add dry ingredients to a mixing bowl
  2. Beat eggs, melt butter and add wet ingredients in another bowl
  3. Once both mixes are slight mixed add them together and mix until there are no chunks
  4. Heat gridle or pan until a drop of water quickly evaporates
  5. Spray gridle or pan with nonstick spray and then add 1/3 cup batter to make pancake
  6. Once all bubble pop flip, the pancake
  7. Serve with toppings of your choice
