Baked Salmon

Next up is one of my favorite dinner items, Baked Salmon!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Fillets of Salmon
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Tamari Sauce

Add Ingredients

Preperation

  1. Heat over to 450 degs F on Broil
  2. Line flat broiling pan with foil
  3. Use tweezers to pull out all of the bones from the fillets
  4. Apply tamari suace to salmon using a spoon
  5. Bake salmon for 13 minutes
  6. Take salmon out to reapply tamari suace and sprinkle sesame seeds
  7. Bake for another 2-3 minutes
  8. Check for doneness
